# 📊 Sales Data Analysis Project

## 📌 Overview
This project focuses on analyzing sales data to extract meaningful insights and support business decision-making. The analysis includes data cleaning, transformation, visualization, and dashboard creation using Google Sheets.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Understand sales performance across different dimensions  
- Identify trends and patterns in sales data  
- Provide actionable business insights  

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Google Sheets  
- Pivot Tables  
- Charts (Line, Bar, Pie)  

---

## 📁 Dataset
The dataset contains sales transaction details including:
- Order Date  
- Product Name  
- Category  
- Region  
- Sales  
- Customer details  

---

## 🔧 Data Cleaning
- Handled missing and inconsistent data  
- Standardized date formats  
- Created a **Month** column for time-based analysis  

---

## 📊 Analysis Performed

### 1. Monthly Sales Analysis
- Analyzed sales trends across months  
- Identified peak and low-performing months  

### 2. Top Products Analysis
- Identified top 5 products based on revenue  
- Observed concentration of sales among few products  

### 3. Category Analysis
- Compared sales across product categories  
- Identified highest and lowest performing categories  

### 4. Region Analysis
- Analyzed sales distribution across regions  
- Identified best and underperforming regions  

---

## 📈 Dashboard
A dashboard was created including:
- Monthly Sales Trend (Line Chart)  
- Sales by Category (Bar Chart)  
- Sales by Region (Pie Chart)  
- Top Products Analysis  
- Total Sales Summary  

---

## 🧠 Key Insights
- Sales peak in November due to seasonal demand  
- Technology category generates the highest revenue  
- West region shows strongest performance  
- A small number of products contribute to majority of sales  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Increase inventory before peak sales periods  
- Improve marketing in low-performing regions  
- Focus on promoting high-demand products  
- Diversify product categories to balance revenue  

---

## 🚀 Conclusion
This project demonstrates the ability to clean, analyze, and visualize data to generate meaningful business insights. It highlights essential data analyst skills such as data handling, trend analysis, and decision-making support.
